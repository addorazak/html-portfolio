The Best Movies According To Abdul-Razak

My top 3 movies of all time.

The Boy Who Harnessed The Wind

This is my favorite movie which is based on a true life story?
A determined young boy in Malawi harnesses the power of wind to save his village from famine.

Guy Ritchie's The Covenant

U.S. Army special operations sergeant John Kinley and his Afghan interpreter Ahmed
form an unlikely alliance as they engage in a dangerous battle against the Taliban in
Guy Ritchie's action-packed thriller, The Covenant

Prison Break

Prison Break follows the thrilling journey of a man who deliberately gets himself incarcerated
to save his wrongfully accused brother and orchestrate an elaborate plan to escape from a
maximum-security prison.
